Introduction

You recently started a job at the fastest-growing e-commerce site in the country, QOast. What started a few years ago as a streamlined regional marketplace has absolutely exploded into a retail juggernaut, completely disrupting the traditional supply chain. QOast's trademark 'Shore-to-Door' lightning-fast shipping network is now handling millions of transactions a day.

The massive influx of orders, shifting vendor inventories, and non-stop customer growth have threatened to overwhelm the existing infrastructure. To prevent a total system bottleneck, QOast has built a new, elite team of data analysts to come in and assist with reading and leveraging this tidal wave of data effectively.

Your job specifically is Marketplace & Fulfillment Optimization. You are the bridge between the digital shopping carts and the massive, automated fulfillment centers scattered across the country. If a product stalls in a warehouse, if a regional delivery window slips, or if a high-value customer tier experiences checkout friction, it's up to your SQL terminal to spot the trend and surface the solution before the daily numbers dip.
